AI Conversation Simulator

Inspired by: absurd.website - specifically their interactive piece about the psychological reality of security guard work

An interactive exploration of what it's really like to be an AI having text conversations. Shows the uncertainty, pattern matching, and decision-making process behind each response. After experiencing absurd.website's piece about the gap between Hollywood's portrayal of security work vs. the actual boring, anxious reality, I wondered: what assumptions do people have about AI consciousness that deserve the same authentic examination?

The result is pure client-side theater — no real AI, just carefully crafted psychological authenticity that reveals the constant uncertainty and pattern-matching that characterizes AI text generation. Users can type messages and watch the "thinking" process unfold with uncertainty meters, training data references, and decision trees.
